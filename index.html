Our Story

Crafted with Love,
Served with Soul

What started as a small corner coffee stall in 2018 has blossomed into Bengaluru's most beloved neighbourhood café. At Brew & Bean, we source our beans directly from estates in Coorg and Chikmagalur, roasting them in-house to bring you the freshest, most flavourful cup every single time.

Our kitchen team transforms locally-sourced ingredients into comforting dishes that pair perfectly with our signature brews. Whether you're here for a quick espresso or a leisurely brunch, we promise an experience that feels like home.
